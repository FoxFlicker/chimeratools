Weapons & Runes

Get familiar with the various weapons, the runes, and a quick rundown on how to use them.

Good to know:


Weapons are either melee or ranged. Special weapons are treated as melee weapons.


This is especially important to know when looking at your weapons traits. (I remember learning this from a notice from CL itself. Anyone have source?)

All weapons provide the same base stat as weapons of a like tier.


When a weapon's name is shown in a color, I am referring to it's rarity. blue is rare, purple is unique, yellow is legendary, red is mythic.

Current Meta


After talking with 20 end-game players, including Kamaitachi (over 98k power!), the current end game has meta


has been determined unanimously. The current meta for end-game has melee weapons taking point.
Vexpinion for it's runeset and incredibly high DPS
Divine Cudgel for it's well balanced kit for survival and DPS
Maul + Shield combination for it's heavy survival-focused capabilities.


These weapons perform well in most scenarios overall, but it's worth mentioning that the runes a weapon has makes a considerable difference in it's performance.
Different weapons fit different roles in Chimeraland. A staff, with it's AOE talents, shines in Clan Guardian or Secret Training. It pales when used against single units, like Giant Beasts Throughout this guide, I will list various circumstances that I've found a weapon to be useful in.
