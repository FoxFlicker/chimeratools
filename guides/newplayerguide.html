New Player's Guide

Let this serve new players as a go-to guide where many of their questions can be answered before they thought to ask.

Character Creation & the first 10 levels...

You're likely not following this guide for your first time playing, so I'm just going to leave a few comments.

  • Don't stress if you don't like avatar you chose. You're able to morph and mix & match any of the races later on.
  • There are no significant differences in abilities. Recipes for the items to unlock these races do vary in difficulty
  • Therefore I recommend choosing a Scaly, Kunze, or Furungulate as their items are the most expensive to craft.
