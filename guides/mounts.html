Premium Mounts

Learn about the premium mounts, which ones to get, and what they can do.

Good to know:


Premium mounts are those that are found in the cash shop, typically released every 2 to 3 weeks.

These mounts provide major boosts to your bloodline and should be prioritized over non-premium mounts.

All premium mounts can fly. Not all of them can swim.


Premium mounts can be obtained by crafting the respective pill at the Elixir Maker, or by purchasing them for rubies in the premium shop.

Obtaining the materials to craft a premium mount is not an easy task. The materials can take weeks or months to farm.

Once you've finally created your mount, you'll need to create various pills to ascend its rarity. This is an expensive and time consuming process.

Therefore, it's recommended you save rubies to purchase the highest ranking pill for your desired mount. This is always the better solution.

DPS

Artigra

Ground | Air | Water

Artigra has been king of Chimeraland since its release alongside Tengu in early 2023. He is unrivaled in AOE and single target damage and he adds a nice lifesteal boost when equipped to your bloodline.

Rating: S

Artigra boasts a heavy DPS in any situation. It's innate lifesteal is a precious gem that helps with survivability for itself and its owner. It can fight in any terrain and has one of the flashiest skill kits in the game. If your goal is to be at the top of the DPS list every time, Artigra is the pet for you.

Sabretooth (Manticore)

Ground | Air

Sabretooth, known as the Manticore in other versions of the game, is a close runner up to the Artigra in terms of raw power.
