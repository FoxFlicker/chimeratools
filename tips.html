General Tips

Easy Raptor Stone Egg Collection

Place a column with a single laminate floor piece on top of it. Stand in the center of it when using your Raptor Stones.
The harriers will not be able to move, and the eggs will all stack in the center making collection simple.


Instant Surface from any Depth

If you're below the water's surface getting back up to the top is as easy at summoning a bone ski from the ride menu.


Interacting with it will instantly propel you to the top of the water.


Utilizing the Fuse Menu

This isn't exactly a cool new trick, but it's something I witness many people not utilizing.

This is the fuse menu. No doubt you'd used it before. But did you know it easily gathers all your like items

Your fuse menu can be utilized in your home location to stack all of your pet/attendant skill items, and your sigils.

This eliminates jumping from storage to storage trying to keep track of your own inventory.

Recommend accessing this menu through a sigil. One too many times have I granted my pet a skill because I was trying to fuse it.

Once you reach this menu, select the left side item and browse the list. Typically, anything that has 3 or more can be fused.


Easily Identify Plot Levels at Base

By using the Renovate feature in Build Mode, you can color your farming plots. Use this to easily distinguish different levels of plots.


Sigil Essence

The level 1 boxes are cheaper than the other two boxes. The amount of level 1 sigils merged to get to the next level are as follows: Level 2: 3 Level 3: 9 Level 4: 27 Level 5: 81 Level 6: 243 Level 7: 729 Total: 1092

Meaning the minimum amount of sigil essence required to max your sigils, assuming you only obtained them by opening boxes of the same level are as follows:

Level 1 Box: 196,830 Sigil Essence.

Level 2 Box: 218,700 Sigil Essence.

Level 3 Box: 218,700 Sigil Essence.

Please note, I ran the numbers about 80 different times using AI, using my own calculations, and with other people. Never once did any of the numbers agree. To the best of my knowledge this is accurate, please inform me if you disagree.
